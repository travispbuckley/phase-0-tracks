1) Push the button located outside the elevator door.
	-Up or down arrow, depending on your destination
2) Enter the elevator once the doors open
3) Once inside, turn 180 degrees and located the panel with buttons with numbers on them
4) Locate the desired floor level, and press the corresponding button with that floor level printed on the front
5) Wait for the doors to close and brace for elevator impact.
6) Once doors re-open, advance out onto the floor.
